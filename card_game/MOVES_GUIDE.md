# 🎮 Memory Card Game - 2 Player Moves Guide

## Game Overview
- **2 Player Competitive Memory Game**
- Players take turns flipping cards to find matching pairs
- Player with the most matches wins!
- All controls are **keyboard-based** only

---

## ⌨️ PLAYER 1 CONTROLS (Red Border Selection)

| Action | Key | Description |
|--------|-----|-------------|
| **Move UP** | `W` | Move card selection upward |
| **Move DOWN** | `S` | Move card selection downward |
| **Move LEFT** | `A` | Move card selection to the left |
| **Move RIGHT** | `D` | Move card selection to the right |
| **Flip Card** | `SPACE` | Select and flip the current card |
| **Rotate Selection** | `R` | Cycle through cards in order |

### Example Player 1 Turn:
1. Press `D` to move right
2. Press `W` to move up
3. Press `SPACE` to flip the card
4. Press `D` again to move to next card
5. Press `SPACE` to flip the second card
6. If cards match, Player 1 gets a point and plays again!

---

## ⌨️ PLAYER 2 CONTROLS (Cyan Border Selection)

| Action | Key | Description |
|--------|-----|-------------|
| **Move UP** | `↑ (Up Arrow)` | Move card selection upward |
| **Move DOWN** | `↓ (Down Arrow)` | Move card selection downward |
| **Move LEFT** | `← (Left Arrow)` | Move card selection to the left |
| **Move RIGHT** | `→ (Right Arrow)` | Move card selection to the right |
| **Flip Card** | `ENTER` | Select and flip the current card |
| **Rotate Selection** | `SHIFT` | Cycle through cards in order |

### Example Player 2 Turn:
1. Press `→` to move right
2. Press `↑` to move up
3. Press `ENTER` to flip the card
4. Press `→` again to move to next card
5. Press `ENTER` to flip the second card
6. If cards match, Player 2 gets a point and plays again!

---

## 🎯 Game Rules

1. **Starting Player**: Player 1 starts first
2. **Turn Flow**: Players alternate turns after flipping 2 cards
3. **Matching**: 
   - If 2 cards match → Current player scores 1 point and plays again
   - If 2 cards don't match → Turn passes to other player
4. **Winning**: Game ends when all 6 pairs are matched. Highest score wins!

## 💡 Tips & Strategies

- **Memory**: Remember where cards are located after flipping
- **Rotate Feature**: Use R (P1) or SHIFT (P2) to quickly cycle through all cards
- **Watch Opponent**: Pay attention to cards your opponent flips
- **Strategic Matching**: Focus on finding pairs you remember
